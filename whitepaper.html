The Journey

The 4 Phases

Each phase delivers value you keep. But the real power is stacking them: each phase feeds the next.

Phase 1: Research & Positioning (Month 1)

The knowledge foundation that powers everything else.

What happens:

  • Deep market research: Competitors, alternatives, ecosystem mapping, market positioning. AI agents comb through news, podcasts, reviews, and case studies to understand the landscape.
  • ICP refinement: Your Ideal Customer Profile gets defined with real data, not assumptions. What size companies? Which industries? Which roles make buying decisions? What problems do they have budget to solve?
  • Positioning and messaging: Validated with friendly customers and real conversations, not pulled from thin air.
  • Asset alignment: Website, pitch decks, LinkedIn profiles, and sales collateral get refreshed so everything speaks the same language.

What you get:

  • A structured knowledge base that AI agents can query so every email, call prep, and reply draws from it
  • Sales assets that land with prospects
  • Clear targeting: who to reach, why they'll care, how to find them
Deliverables: Market research report, ICP documentation, positioning framework, refreshed sales assets. All yours to keep.
CairnShift · 2026

Phase 2: Activation & Tooling (Months 2-3)

The engine that finds and activates high-fit prospects.

What happens:

  • Tiered enrichment: A budget-protecting waterfall. Cheap firmographic checks first, then AI validation for qualitative fit, then deep research to filter out high value targets.
  • Ghost Profile discovery: Besides our 25 data provides the engine can find persona's who aren't active on LinkedIn by scraping company sites, blogs, forums, and news.
  • Multi-source activation: Act on real buying signals: intent data showing who's researching solutions like yours, event attendance, funding announcements, job changes, social engagement. Reach out when there's a reason, not randomly.
  • Campaign execution: Continuous A/B testing of messaging and channels. What works gets scaled, what doesn't gets killed.

What you get: A qualified pipeline of prospects who have reasons to care, and infrastructure that scales.

Deliverables: Prospect activation system, enriched target lists, campaign templates, performance dashboard.

Phase 3: Sales Execution (Months 3-5)

Scaling deal flow with AI-augmented workflow.

What happens:

  • Reply Agent: When a prospect replies, AI pulls full conversation history, queries your knowledge base, and drafts a context-aware response. Generated drafts appear in your drafts folder for review, regardless of your email client.
  • Call Prep Agent: Delivers a briefing before every meeting: conversation history, company research, 3 to 5 hooks based on your sales playbook, recommended talking points. Reply to the email to ask follow-up questions. It has full context loaded.

  • Post-Meeting Agent: Every interaction gets transcribed, whether online, phone, or even voice notes from in-person meetings. You get follow-up email drafts, researched answers to unanswered questions, and next-step recommendations. The agent adapts to deal stage: detailed follow-ups after discovery calls, draft proposals after technical deep-dives.
  • Sales playbook development: Our team co-runs the first discovery calls. What works gets documented and built into a structured playbook. When objections come up, responses get added. When new patterns emerge, the playbook gets updated.
  • Multi-channel re-activation: If email goes cold, shift to LinkedIn. If LinkedIn doesn't work, try WhatsApp or phone. Never lose a deal because you got stuck in one blocked channel.

What you get:

  • Sales reps moving faster with less time on admin and more time selling
  • Higher close rates from better prep, faster follow-up, and consistent messaging
  • Playbooks that new hires can follow from day one
  • A system that learns from every interaction
Deliverables: AI agents embedded in your workflow, documented sales playbooks, call transcription system, multi-channel orchestration.
